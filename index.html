FLAT STANLEY VISITS UMD!



Aaron's Flat Stanley visited Jenny (Aaron's Godmother) at the University of Maryland. Stanley got to live with a college student from September to November 2019! Check out some of the fun things that he did while he was in Maryland!




About University of Maryland:

University of Maryland first opened in 1856. UMD is located between Washington D.C. and Baltimore. There are about 30,000 students that attend the university and the mascot is a terrapin (a type of turtle) named Testudo.


  • Stanley learned how to code!

    • Stanley became a temporary student at the University of Maryland, where he got the oppotunity to study computer science. He learned how to code and even helped create this website!






  • Stanley became a Peer Mentor!

    • As a peer mentor, he worked at the front desk of the Computer Science department. He also helped students plan their schedules and choose what classes they want to take.






  • Stanley sat with Jim Henson and Kermit the Frog!

    • Jim Henson is the creator of the Muppets. He also attended the University of Maryland. We have a statue of him on campus to celebrate his accomplishments!






  • Stanley took a train to New York City!
